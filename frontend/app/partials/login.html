Write Unlimited Polls

Ask any question you want to the Princeton community. Need to know what class to take next semester? Which professor is the most forgiving? Which eating club has the best Dean's Date? Write a poll, list as many options as you want, and get answers from your classmates.

Get Instant Feedback

Anyone with a Princeton email can answer a poll. Share links with your friends to see what they think or browse the feed to see what other people are asking. Get statistics on how your poll is doing, what the most popular responses are, and response demographics.

Stay Anonymous

No one on PollPrinceton can know who published a poll other than its author. Votes are anonymous too. Ask whatever you want, without fear of judgment.
